About Paws Haven

A rescue shelter dedicated to giving abandoned animals a second chance at life.

Our Story

Paws Haven began with a small group of volunteers who believed that every animal deserves love, safety, and a place to call home. What started as a community effort to rescue stray animals quickly grew into a dedicated shelter focused on rehabilitation, medical care, and responsible adoption.

Over the years, Paws Haven has rescued hundreds of dogs and cats from difficult situations. With the help of volunteers, donors, and veterinarians, we provide shelter, nourishment, and compassion to animals while we search for families willing to open their hearts and homes.

Our mission is simple: rescue animals in need, care for them responsibly, and match them with loving forever homes.
